## SLIDE CONTENT

### 🔴 TITLE: LockBit 3.0 — The Dominant Ransomware Threat

#### KEY STATISTICS (Left Column)
- **30-35%** market share — largest ransomware threat
- **$1B+** annual global damage
- **5,000+** organizations targeted annually
- Active since 2019 | Highly operational (Feb 2026)

#### THREAT PROFILE (Center Column)
- **Encryption**: RSA-4096 + AES-256 (mathematically unbreakable)
- **Speed**: Encrypt millions of files in hours
- **Model**: Sophisticated affiliate program (3-tier)
- **Operations**: Professional, well-funded, organized

#### IMMEDIATE IMPACT (Right Column)
- Initial Access → Encryption: **10-14 days**
- Ransom Demand: **$1M - $20M+**
- Detection Window: **30-60 days** (without advanced controls)
- Operational Loss: **$500k-$5M/hour**

#### CALL TO ACTION (Bottom)
**DEFENSIVE PRIORITY:** Deploy EDR, Enable MFA, Test Backups
**OFFENSIVE FOCUS:** Understand affiliate networks, detect C2 comms

## SPEAKER NOTES (Read this while showing the slide)

### Opening (15 seconds)
"LockBit 3.0 is the single most dangerous ransomware threat today. One in three ransomware incidents are LockBit attacks. We need to understand what makes it so effective."

### Key Threat (30 seconds)
"Two things make LockBit unstoppable: First, the encryption—they use military-grade RSA-4096 and AES-256, no known breaks. Second, the business model—they've created a professional affiliate program that scales their operations across industries globally."

### Business Model (20 seconds)
"They're not script kiddies. They operate like a legitimate business. Initial Access Brokers get 25-30% cut. Red teamers doing lateral movement get 15-20%. Encryption operators get 15-20%. LockBit leadership keeps 20-30%. This is sustainable, organized, professional crime."

### Impact (25 seconds)
"Speed matters. They move from initial access to full encryption in roughly 2 weeks. They steal data for extortion WHILE encrypting. If we don't detect them in that window—and most organizations don't—the impact is devastating. One hospital lost $24 million in 72 hours."

### What We Must Do (20 seconds)
"For defenders: EDR on every endpoint, MFA on remote access, and air-gapped backups—those three things stop 80% of successful attacks. For offensive operators: understand their C2 infrastructure, map their affiliate networks, and support law enforcement operations."

### Closing (10 seconds)
"LockBit will likely release version 4.0 this year. The threat is evolving. We need to invest in detection and prevention now, not after an incident."
